# ASCII Art Candidates

Curated from [asciiart.eu](https://www.asciiart.eu/) animal, mythology, plant, space, and nature collections.
Filters applied: 4-18 lines tall, max 60 chars wide, deduplicated, max 8 per form.
Some pieces have artist initials embedded (`sb`, `dwb`, `ldb`, `jgs`, `Caffrey`, etc). **Preserve these when adopting** .  these are signed works.

**Total forms:** 31 | **Total pieces:** 194

---

## How to use this file

1. Read through the candidates per form.
2. Pick the ones you want in the grove.
3. For each picked piece, save it to `templates/forms/<form>.txt` in the summoning repo.
4. The wizard renders `{{NAME}}` as a placeholder. If the piece has signature initials, leave them in.
5. Update `gallery.txt` with `keywords:filename:description` if it's a new form.
